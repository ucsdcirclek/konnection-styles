I belive in Paradise!

UCSD Circle K is a part of Paradise Division, one of the eight nine divisions in Cal-Nev-Ha (see District tab). Our current Lieutenant Governor is San Diego State University's Amanda Spendlove Gerald Biando. Our division comprises seven eight schools that range in location from California to Hawaii. Paradise Division's mascots is are a toucan and a pineapple.
