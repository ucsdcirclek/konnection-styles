Fall Training Conference

Fall Training Conference, also known as FTC, is known by some as the best event of the year. Taking place during fall quarter Fall Quarter in the mountains of Sonora, CA., FTC has something for new and returning members regardless of how many times they've attended. Every year, hundreds of Circle K'ers travel far and wide all the way up to the mountains of Sonora to attend the magic that is Fall Training Conference. The event is a great opportunity for new members to meet and interact with the other Circle K clubs in the California-Nevada-Hawaii district. Members attend informational workshops put on by their peers on every subject from Circle K 101 to Star Gazing, then relax by watching campfire skits. As if all that were not enticing enough, there's even a great dance put on at the end of the night, where Circle K'ers show off their wild side! Check out photos from 2010's FTC here (https://ucsdcirclekphotoalbum.shutterfly.com/2010-2011/1668).
